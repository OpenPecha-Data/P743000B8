|| 1 ||

svam ātmānaṃ darśanaṃ hi tat tam eva na paśyati |na paśyati yad ātmānaṃ kathaṃ drakṣyati tat
parān || 2 ||

na paryāpto ’gnidṛṣṭānto darśanasya prasiddhaye |sadarśanaḥ sa pratyukto gamyamānagatāgataiḥ
|| 3 ||

nāpaśyamānaṃ bhavati yadā kiṃ cana darśanam |darśanaṃ paśyatīty evaṃ katham etat tu yujyate
|| 4 ||

paśyati darśanaṃ naiva naiva paśyaty adarśanam |vyākhyāto darśanenaiva draṣṭā cāpy avagamyatām
|| 5 ||

asati te kutaḥ || 6 ||

|| 7 ||
